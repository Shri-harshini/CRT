Angela's Recipe for the Best

Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk

  • 2 teaspoon yeast

  • 1/4 cup granulates sugar

  • 1 egg plus

  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup of dark brown sugar

  • 1/4 cup butter

  • 1 1/2 tablespoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. mix the milk

  2. melt the butter

  3. add in the flour

  4. Knead the dough

  5. transfer the dough
